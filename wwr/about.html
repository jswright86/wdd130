History

Founded over 20 years ago by Captain Jack and his wife Amber, Rafting Adventures has become well known for the experiences we bring to our customers. Captain Jack and Amber started the company with a simple idea: to share their love of rafting with others. They had both grown up in Idaho, surrounded by the beautiful rivers and mountains that make this area so special. With a deep-rooted knowledge of the rivers and a passion for adventure and a love for the outdoors, they set out to create a company that would provide unforgettable experiences for families and individuals alike.

Over the years, they have built a team of experienced guides who share their passion for rafting and the great outdoors. Together, they have created a company that is dedicated to safety, fun, and adventure. Today, Rafting Adventures is known for its commitment to excellence and its reputation for providing the best rafting experience in all of Idaho. With a focus on customer satisfaction and a dedication to safety, Rafting Adventures has become the go-to choice for families and individuals looking for an unforgettable rafting experience.
