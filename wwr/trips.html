Rafting Trips

We offer several excursion on both the Snake and Salmon Rivers, ranging from half-day to multi-day trips. Our most popular trips are on the Salmon River(aka the "River of No Return"), which is known for its stunning scenery and thrilling rapids. We also offer trips on the Middle Fork of the Salmon River, which is a more remote and rugged experience and is considered by many as one of the best rafting trips in the country.

There are also trips on the Snake River, which is a great option for families and beginners. The Snake River has a variety of rapids, ranging from Class I to Class III, making it a great choice for all skill levels.

We also offer trips on the Hells Canyon, which is the deepest river gorge in North America. This trip is perfect for those looking for a more adventurous experience, as it features some of the most challenging rapids in the country. No matter what type of trip you choose, you can expect breathtaking scenery, thrilling rapids, and an unforgettable experience.

Rapid Classifations

Class I: Easy, moving water with small waves. Suitable for beginners.

Class II: Moderate rapids with some waves and obstacles. Suitable for beginners with some experience.

Class III: Intermediate rapids with larger waves and more obstacles. Suitable for experienced rafters.

Class IV: Advanced rapids with large waves and significant obstacles. Suitable for expert rafters.

Class V: Expert rapids with extreme conditions and hazards. Suitable for expert rafters only.

It's important to consider the rapid classifications on the trip you take. Safety is our number one priority. If you have little to no experience with white water, our beginner and family trips might be a better option to get your feet wet.
